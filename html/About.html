Lendu E-sport Arena or known as Lensa is a UiTM Alor Gajah event hosted by MC111 students. It encourages Malaysians to participate in online gaming tournaments such as Valorant, Mobile Legends Bang Bang (ML) , and Playerunknown's Battlegrounds (PUBG). Furthermore, the Lensa event occurs once a year, and this year for Lensa22 will be conducted in a physical location after being held online last year due to the Covid19.
